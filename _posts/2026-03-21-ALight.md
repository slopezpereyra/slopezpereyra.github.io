---
title: This life of mine 
categories: [Poesía]
---

> And the light shineth in darkness; and the darkness comprehended it not.<br>
>— John 1:5


This life of mine passed through the world as bare<br>
as only void can be. But then a flare<br>
cast out a light towards a final summit.<br>
And darkness—darkness cannot overcome it.
