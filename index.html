This is a sundry collection of scientific notes, philosophical and personal considerations, and literature. Since an early age, I have made of writing a habit, and the principal occupation of my solitude. Such practice, at times ritualistic, but more often than not austere and mundane, brings with it a strange proliferation of orphan, bastard, or unlawful writings, which lack a propper place to be, and have no addressees nor friends, and yet stubbornly dream of being read albeit once. It is such proliferation that I here collect.
