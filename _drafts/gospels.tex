From J. Ellul \textit{Anarchy and Christianity}:

\begin{quote}
There has always been a Christian anarchism. In every century there have been
Christians who have discovered the simple biblical truth, whether
intellectually, mystically, or socially. They include some great names, for
example, Tertullian (at first), Fra Dolcino, Francis of Assisi, Wycliffe, Luther
(except for the twofold mistake of putting power back in the hands of the
princes and supporting the massacre of rebellious peasants), Lammenais, John
Bost, and Charles de Foucauld.
\end{quote}
